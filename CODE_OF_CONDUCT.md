# Code of Conduct

OpenForge is a public lab. Be respectful, professional, and constructive.

## Our Standards
- Be kind and direct.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on the work, not the person.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or abuse.
- Trolling or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.

## Enforcement
Project maintainers may remove content or restrict access to protect the community.
